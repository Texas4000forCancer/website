About Us

Our Story and Mission

Who We Are

Texas 4000 for Cancer is a nonprofit organization dedicated to raising funds and awareness for cancer research and support services. Through our signature cycling events, we bring together passionate individuals to make a real difference in the fight against cancer.

Our team of dedicated riders, volunteers, and supporters work tirelessly to create meaningful experiences that inspire change and generate critical funding for cancer organizations.

Our Mission

What Drives Us

🎯

Raise Awareness

We create unforgettable cycling experiences that spark conversations and increase public awareness about cancer prevention and support.

💰

Generate Funding

Every mile matters. We channel our passion for cycling into meaningful fundraising efforts that directly support cancer research and patient care.

🤝

Build Community

Our events bring together riders, volunteers, and supporters who share a common goal: making a difference in the lives of those affected by cancer.

❤️

Support Survivors

We're committed to supporting cancer survivors and their families throughout their journey, providing hope and resources when they need them most.
